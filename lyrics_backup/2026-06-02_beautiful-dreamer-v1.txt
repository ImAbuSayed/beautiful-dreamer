[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Wake up the stars just to watch you shine
I've been waiting all night for your hand in mine
Every breath you take is a song I have known
In the quiet of the dark, we are not alone

[Verse 1]
There's a soft southern breeze blowing off the pine
And your lips taste sweet like a cherry wine
I can feel the heat in the shadows we cast
We got a slow-burning fire and it's meant to last

[Pre-Chorus]
I lean in close so I can see you smile
I want to stay right here for a little while
I trace a map of the world on your skin
We don't need to know where the night will begin

[Chorus]
Beautiful dreamer, let the world slip away
We can hide in the moon till the break of day
Wrap your arms around my restless soul
You are the only piece making me whole

[Post-Chorus]
[Background Vocal] (Ah, let it slip away)
Hide in the moon till the break of day

[Verse 2]
The radio is playing a gentle tune
And we dance right here underneath the moon
Every step we take pulls me deeper in
To the beautiful trouble of where we've been

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The whiskey is gone but we still feel the spin
I don't want to stop now that we can begin
So lock up the doors and pull down the blind
I want to get lost in the back of your mind

[Chorus]
Beautiful dreamer, let the world slip away
We can hide in the moon till the break of day
Wrap your arms around my restless soul
You are the only piece making me whole

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Background Vocal] (Ah, let it slip away)
Hide in the moon till the break of day

[Bridge]
Let the shadows fall around us slow
Watch the heavy memories turn and go
Hold me in the center of the fire
Pull me deeper into sweet desire
Even when the morning starts to bleed
You will have the beating heart of Saeed

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (I never knew a love could feel this deep)
[Spoken] (Just close your eyes and let the world fall asleep)

[Final Chorus]
Beautiful dreamer, let the world slip away
We can hide in the moon till the break of day
Wrap your arms around my restless soul
Beautiful dreamer, you finally made me whole
